# Social Media Expert (TikTok) Hiring Packet - Recall.bio

## Role Mandate
Own TikTok growth execution for Recall.bio in partnership with design. The role converts user stories (daily legacy journaling via text, audio, and images) into a repeatable content engine that drives awareness, installs, and retained creation behavior.

## 1) Hiring Criteria

### Must-Have Competencies
- 3+ years operating TikTok growth for a consumer app, creator brand, or content product.
- Demonstrated wins with short-form content systems: ideation, scripting, production briefs, posting, and iteration cadence.
- Working knowledge of TikTok API capabilities/limits, including publishing workflows, analytics extraction, and policy constraints.
- Strong creative direction skills with practical collaboration habits for designer/editor partnerships.
- Data fluency: can define hypotheses, run content experiments, read retention/CTR/completion metrics, and decide next actions.

### Preferred Competencies
- Experience in journaling, memory, family, legacy, wellness, or emotionally resonant consumer products.
- UGC sourcing and creator-collaboration experience.
- Familiarity with lifecycle marketing handoff from social traffic to activation.

### Disqualifiers
- Portfolio is aesthetic-only with no measurable growth outcomes.
- No evidence of test-and-learn process or experimentation rhythm.
- Cannot explain a TikTok metric framework beyond vanity views.

## 3) Interview Rubric (100 points)

- TikTok strategy depth (25)
- Creative execution quality (20)
- Analytics and experimentation rigor (20)
- Cross-functional collaboration with design/product (15)
- Recall.bio mission alignment and storytelling sensitivity (10)
- Operational reliability and communication (10)

### Practical Case (Required)
Prompt: Build a 14-day TikTok content plan for Recall.bio with three content pillars, post-level hypotheses, KPI targets, and an iteration loop.

Evaluation focus:
- Quality of hooks and narrative format.
- Plausibility of test matrix and KPI targets.
- Ability to adapt content based on day 3 and day 7 hypothetical performance snapshots.

## 4) Onboarding Plan (First 30 Days)

### Days 1-3
- Product immersion: create and publish a personal memory entry using text/audio/image flows.
- Message architecture review: emotional promise, user pain points, proof assets.
- Align with designer on operating cadence and approval workflow.

### Week 1
- Audit current social footprint and baseline metrics.
- Finalize content pillars and production template library.
- Launch first 5 posts with pre-defined hypotheses.

### Week 2
- Implement analytics dashboard and review rhythm (daily tactical, weekly strategic).
- Run at least 2 A/B experiments on hook + format structure.
- Establish creator/UGC collaboration shortlist.

### Weeks 3-4
- Scale to consistent posting cadence with documented playbook.
- Present month-one insights: winners, losers, and next 30-day plan.
- Define handoff interface to product/CRM for activation and retention loops.

## Success Metrics (First 30 Days)
- Posting cadence met (minimum agreed weekly output).
- Improvement in view-through/completion and saves/shares trend.
- At least one repeatable content format identified.
- Clear monthly growth plan tied to Recall.bio business outcomes.

## Operational Constraint
Board-level access is required to complete final in-platform hiring action (agent creation). All strategy and execution deliverables are prepared; final hire cannot be finalized by CEO runtime permission alone.
