## Design Context

### Users
Individuals intent on documenting their life story and memories (text, audio, video) to create an emotional archive and digital legacy for their families. They typically use the app daily to capture reflections.

### Brand Personality
Voice and tone are **calm & reflective**.
Personality in three words/phrases: **modern editorial**, **earthy minimalism**, and **grounded sophistication**.
Emotional goals: Creating a sense of **digital heritage**.

### Aesthetic Direction
Theme: **Light mode exclusively**.
Visual Tone: Soft, warm, organic styling using earthy and terracotta colors (`primary-terracotta`, `primary-clay`, `accent-sage` etc.) with soft decorative blobs to add a calming, reflective feel.

### Design Principles
1. **Focus on the Essence**: Employ earthy minimalism; strip away unnecessary interface clutter so the emotional narrative stands out.
2. **Modern Editorial Layouts**: Ensure all documented memories, both text and media, are presented with sophisticated typography and spacing, giving a legacy-worthy feel.
3. **Calm & Reflective Environment**: Foster peace of mind through a warm, organic color palette. Avoid loud interactions.
4. **Light Mode Excellence**: Direct 100% of styling and thematic refinements into the light mode viewing experience, ignoring dark mode.
